Percy Jackson and the Lightning Thief

Do you love greek mythology, thrilling adventures, and witty sarcasm, then you will love reading this book!

Percy Jackson and the Lightning Thief is a about a boy named Percy, a young demigod, who had been accused of steal one of Zeus's most prized item, the master bolt. In order to clear his name, he must go on a quest with his new friends, Annabeth and Grover, to find the master bolt and give it back to Zeus in just a short amount of time. Along the way, he encounters multiple monsters and obstacles that challenge him and his friends, testing their skills, strengths, and weaknesses. And in the process, he starts to discover who he truly is.
